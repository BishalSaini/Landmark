About us

Landmark Infrastructure Solutions Pvt Ltd is serving the infra development and industrial capacity expansion needs of India through its two verticals: Construction Equipment Rental, and Turnkey Infra-Project Execution for three decades now.

Our team of expert professionals and young well-maintained fleet of equipment are serving sectors like Power, Oil & Gas, Steel, Cement, Renewable Energy, and Urban & Rural Infrastructure development such as Roads, Bridges, Highways, Airports, Metros, Irrigation Projects & Water Pipelines.

The company’s fleet has been meticulously planned to meet the changing demands of its clients’ needs. It has 100+ equipment that include cranes up to 800 MT capacity, with a good mix of Crawlers and All-Terrain Cranes, Hydraulic Piling Rigs and Concrete Equipment like Batching Plants and Mobile Boom Placers of up to 46 meters.


Our turnkey division is engaged in catering to Renewable energy sector where we take contracts of WTG erections and girders transportation and launching work for metro & bridges, setting of dedicated RMC plants for supplying & pumping of concrete.


At Landmark, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality equipment and unparalleled service to our valued clients. Our extensive fleet of state-of-the-art machinery, including crawler &tyre mounted cranes, concrete boom placer, transit mixers, batching plants & self-propelled & truck mounted boom lifts, and more, is meticulously maintained to ensure optimal performance and safety on every job site.
